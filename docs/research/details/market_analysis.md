## Market Difficulty Rankings

### EASIEST Markets (Best for Value)
1. **Under 2.5 Goals** - Low-scoring leagues (Ligue 1, Serie A defensive)
2. **BTTS NO** - Strong defense vs weak attack
3. **Draws** - High in low-scoring leagues
4. **Correct Score (1-0, 0-0)** - High variance but valuable

### MODERATE Markets
1. **BTTS YES** - Best: odds 1.85-2.20 with 10%+ edge
2. **Over 2.5 Goals** - High-scoring leagues (Bundesliga, Eredivisie)
3. **1X2** - Hardest to beat (bookmakers good at these)

### HARDEST Markets (Avoid)
1. **Short-odds favorites** (1.0-2.0x) - Even with high win rate, LOSES money
2. **Correct Score** - High variance, hard to model
3. **Asian Handicap** - Complex, bookmakers expert

## Findings by Market

### 1X2 (Match Result) - HARDEST
- Bookmakers have sophisticated models
- 50-55% accuracy is good
- Less edge available
- Better for favorites at home

### Over/Under 2.5 - MODERATE
- OVER 2.5: Best in Bundesliga (60-65% hit), Eredivisie
- UNDER 2.5: Best in Ligue 1, Serie A defensive teams
- 50/50 market at ~1.85 odds
- Easier than 1X2

### BTTS - EASIER
- **Optimal odds**: 1.85-2.20 range
- **Sweet spot**: 10%+ edge required
- **Backtest**: +37.84 units, +4.29% ROI, 54.65% win rate
- Best leagues: Serie B (+63.9%), Croatia HNL (+70.6%), Japan J1 (100%)
- Premier League bookmaker overprices slightly (implied 58% vs actual 52%)

### BTTS + Over 2.5 Combo - HIGH VALUE
- Odds 2.00-2.50
- Both conditions: BTTS Yes + 3+ goals
- Popular accumulator leg

### Correct Score - HARDEST
- High variance
- Best for selective use only
- Target: 1-1, 2-1, 2-0 patterns in low-scoring leagues
- Use as small portion of portfolio

### From Kingspredict
- Match profile determines market:
  - Open game → Over 2.5 or BTTS
  - Strong home → BTTS & Win
  - Defensive → Under 2.5
