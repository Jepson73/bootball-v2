## First Half / Second Half Analysis

### Key Findings

**Most goals scored in FIRST HALF:**
- High-pressure teams
- Liverpool 1H: undefeated 2021/22
- Bayern 1H: 78% scoring rate

**After first goal:**
- 65% chance another goal before half-time
- Momentum shifts create second-half opportunities

### First Half Markets Strategy
- **Over 0.5 1H**: Better than full-time for some leagues
- **BTTS 1H**: Often underpriced by bookmakers
- Team dominates 1H but leads 1-0 → opposing team pushes = BTTS 2H

### Second Half Patterns
- 90+ minutes: Australia scores often in added time
- Late goals (80-90 min) more common in:
  - MLS (fitness)
  - A-League (endurance)
  - Bundesliga (pace)

## Market Variations

### 1. Standard BTTS
- **Yes/No**: Both teams to score in 90 min
- **Odds**: 1.60-2.00
- **Best**: Odds 1.85-2.20 with 10%+ edge

### 2. BTTS & Win (COMBO)
- Both teams score + specific team wins
- **Odds**: 3.00-4.00
- Strong home team + leaky defense = value

### 3. BTTS Both Halves
- Both score in 1H AND 2H
- **Minimum**: 2-2 final score
- **Odds**: 8.00-15.00 (HIGH RISK)
- Rare: ~10% of matches
- Use: Cup games, derbies only

### 4. BTTS 1H / 2H
- **BTTS 1H**: Score in first 45 min
- **BTTS 2H**: Score in second 45 min
- **Strategy**: Teams that score early OR late consistently

### 5. BTTS + Over 2.5 (COMBO)
- BTTS Yes + Over 2.5 goals
- **Odds**: 2.00-2.50
- Most popular accumulator leg
- Correlated markets = sometimes mispriced

### 6. Over/Under Variations
- **Over 1.5**: Very safe (75%+ hit rates)
- **Over 2.5**: The standard (~50%)
- **Over 3.5**: High variance, high reward
- **Under 2.5**: Ligue 1, Serie A defensive

## BTTS + Over 1.5 Combo Analysis

### The Relationship

Research from GoalStatistics shows:
- When BTTS = Yes, 80% of time Over 2.5 also hits
- BUT Over 2.5 doesn't require BTTS (2-0 wins)
- **Over 1.5 is safer**: Needs only 2 goals total

### BTTS + Over 1.5 Logic

If BTTS is in (both teams score), Over 1.5 automatically wins IF:
- 1-1 final score
- 2-1, 3-2, etc.

Actually:
- **BTTS Yes + Over 1.5 Yes** = Any 2+ goal outcome where both score
- **BTTS Yes + Over 1.5 No** = Impossible (can't have BTTS without 2 goals)
- So these are CORRELATED, not additional

### Better Combinations
- **BTTS Yes** is primary pick
- **Over 1.5** is safer secondary (lower variance)
- Both hit when both teams are scoring consistently

### When to Pick Each
- **Over 1.5**: Low-risk matches, defensive teams, uncertain games
- **BTTS**: High-scoring league matches, attacking teams
- **Both**: High-correlation - pick ONE, not both
